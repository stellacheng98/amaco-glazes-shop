Our Story

Glazes you can actually try

We're potters who got tired of spending $20 on a pint of glaze only to discover it wasn't quite the color we imagined after firing. So we started bottling 4 oz samples of the AMACO mid-high fire glazes we love most.

A 4 oz jar gives you enough glaze to test on multiple tiles, experiment with layering, and really understand how a color behaves in your kiln — without the full commitment of a pint or quart.

Every jar is filled fresh from factory-sealed AMACO containers, properly mixed, and labeled with the exact product code so you can order the full size with confidence.

FAQ

What cone range do these glazes fire to?
AMACO mid-high fire glazes are formulated for cone 5–10 (approximately 2185°F–2345°F). Always test a sample tile first in your specific kiln and atmosphere.
How much does 4 oz cover?
Roughly 3–4 standard 4×4 test tiles at 3 coats, or one small mug exterior. More than enough to evaluate a color and layering behavior.
How should I store my sample?
Keep the lid tight and store at room temperature away from freezing. Stir or shake well before each use — settling is normal.
Are these the same glazes AMACO sells?
Yes — we purchase directly from AMACO and portion into our own jars. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by AMACO.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently US-only due to shipping restrictions on liquid goods. We're working on expanding this.
